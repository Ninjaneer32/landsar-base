Instructions:

This form focuses on information collected by the search team at the conclusion of a search effort. Where possible, the AAR measurements are compliant with the International Search & Rescue Incident Database (ISRID) data collection guidelines. The information you enter, along with relevant details of the search actions taken using LandSAR, will help us to improve LandSAR's predictive algorithms. You should not include any personally identifiable information (PII) in your responses.
